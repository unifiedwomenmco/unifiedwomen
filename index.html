About Us

Welcome to our women-only cooperative organization! We are a collective enterprise of women who have come together to improve our economic and social status. Our organization operates on the principles of mutual aid, self-help, and solidarity, where we pool our resources and skills to create a business or service that benefits the entire group.

Our decision-making is democratic and based on consensus-building, and we strive to provide our members with economic opportunities, financial security, and a supportive community of women. We have been successful in various industries, such as agriculture, handicrafts, and services, and we use our organization as a tool for promoting gender equality and women's empowerment.

Join us to be a part of a community of like-minded women who are committed to creating economic opportunities for ourselves and our communities. Together, we can make a difference!
